# Work IQ Signals

Synthetic signal definitions used by the Syntrix cinematic demo.

## Signals

- Interaction frequency
- Prompt type
- Estimated time saved
- Repetition score
- Business value score
- Risk score
- Week 1 vs Week 3 opportunity lift

## Supported Demo Profiles

- Marketing Manager
- Project Manager
- HR Business Partner

## Boundary

All signals come from local CSV files in `synthetic_data/`. No real Copilot logs, graph data, employee records, customer records, or email content are used.
