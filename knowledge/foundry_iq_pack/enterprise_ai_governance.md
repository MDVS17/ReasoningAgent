# Enterprise AI Governance

## Purpose

This synthetic source describes governance expectations for local Syntrix demo agents.

## Controls

- Require human approval before external communication.
- Require human approval before changes to systems of record.
- Flag HR, legal, financial, customer-impacting, and employee-impacting content.
- Keep source traceability for every recommendation.
- Keep write actions disabled unless explicitly approved by a human.

## Review Standard

Governed agents should be transparent, auditable, and limited to the workflow they were designed to support.
